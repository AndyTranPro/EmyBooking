The Dashboard (HDR Student)

Timetable View

Above is the timetable view. There are three functionailites in the sub nav:

  • The > will allow you to see bookings for the next date
  • Level Two is a select dropdown to choose a different floor.
  • Map View will switch the dashboard from the timetable view to the map view (described below)

    • The > on the right side of the page will allow you to see bookings for more rooms.

    The funnel icon on the left side of the page will allow you to filter for different times and properties of the room.

Map View

The green buttons are rooms that you have permissions to book. This will depend on your role at CSE.

You can switch back to the timetable view in the subnav, as well as view more levels / choose another date to book.

Clicking on a button will highlight that room in the timetable view.

Making a booking

To make a booking, click on an available cell to make a booking, and fill in the details in the modal that pops up.

You cannot make a booking that starts before the current hour!

Deleting a booking

You can now view the booking on the timetable.

Click on the trash icon, when viewing your booking, then 'Delete' again to confirm your deletion.

This can also be done in the MyBookings tab.

My Bookings

Checking In

To Check into a booking, click on the corresponding accordian and then click the green 'Check In' button.

Note: You can only check into a booking within 15 minutes of the start time.

After Checking in, the accordian will be green, and you will be checked in!

Exporting to calendar

Clicking Export All ICS File, will create a file in your browsers download

You can import this file to any standard calender app (built in Windows, Apple, Google Calender, etc.) to add the events to your calendar.

Non CSE Staff

Making Requests

To make a request as a Non-CSE staff, it is the same process as making a booking as a different type of user. Note that you can only view meeting rooms if you are non-cse staff.

Viewing Requests

Head to the MyBookings tab to see status on your request:

  • Red = Denied
  • Yellow = Pending
  • Appears as Booking = Accepted

Admin Functionalities

Accepting / Denying Requests

To Review Request, click on the 'Requests' Tab.

Click on the corresponding accoridian, and click 'Accept' or 'Deny'

Viewing / Generating Reports

To generate usage reports, head to the 'Reports' tab, choose 2 dates and click 'Generate Report'

Booking on behalf of staff

When making a booking as an admin, you can optionally select the booking to be on behalf of another person.

Deleting Other's bookings

As an admin, you can click any booking on the timetable, and delete the booking via the Trash can Icon.

Invalid Bookings

After attempting to book in an invalid way, an error message will appear on the bottom left of screen, and no booking will be made.

The following cases are invalid:

  • Booking two rooms at the same time (hdr student)
  • Making a booking that overlaps with another booking
  • Booking for longer than 8 hours
